Temprature



To convert celsuis to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the 'c' with your temprature in celsuis: (c x 9/5) + 32
Weight



To convert Kilogram to pound yourself, use this formula replacing the 'kg' with your weight in kilograms: kg x 2.205
Distance



To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilmeters: km ÷ 1.609
